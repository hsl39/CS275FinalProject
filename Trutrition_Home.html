WHAT WE DO HERE:

Here at Trutrition, our goal is to allow to to keep a comprehensive diary, enabling you to personally keep track of any foods you wish to log for nutritional purposes.




HOW IT WORKS:

Nutritional Lookup

If you want to find the nutritional facts for any type of food you might be interested in,
you simply need to enter your desired food into the box provided, pressing search will then produce a list of nutritional facts compiled from the USDA regarding your desired food.
Our service makes a request to the USDA database, which then returns to us all of the nutritional facts they can find from that entry. If you're at all curious about the USDA's nutritional information, you can visit their site by clicking the image below.

Food Diary

In order to create your food diary, simply select a date and write in what food you ate and what time you ate it. It will then be logged in the diary.
